About Anime and Games

Anime and games have cured my life. Below, I would like to introduce some of my favorite anime and games to you.


神様はじめました

This photo was taken at the famous scenic spot Wenheyou in Changsha.The city is composed of Sexytea,crayfish and secretroom escape.This 《託された思い》 is my favorite music in this anime.



ハイキュー！

《ハイキュー！》is my favorite type of sports anime.It tells the passionate story of a group of teenagers constantly surpassing themselves while playing volleyball.This 《FLY HIGH!!!》 is my favorite music in this anime.



あつまれ どうぶつの森

《あつまれ どうぶつの森》is a healing game that I really like.In this game, I live on a deserted island with animals, making the island more prosperous.The pictures are screenshots of me playing the game.



Persona 5

《Persona 5》is known as the world's top JRPG style game.In this game, you experience the daily life of a Japanese male high school student and work with friends to change Japanese society.
